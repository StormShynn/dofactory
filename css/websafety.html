Websafe colors consist of 216 colors that any device will be able to display.

Websafe fonts are fonts that are available on any browser and any device.

They are safe because they are guaranteed to be supported.

Websafe Colors

#

This list of colors was defined in 1996 when computers had 8-bit graphic cards.

Today, this list is less important as most devices offer rich graphics.
